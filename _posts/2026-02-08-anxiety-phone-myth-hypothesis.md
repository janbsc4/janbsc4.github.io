>It is also possible that phones serve as a good/useful “myth” (I mean this in a positive sense) for the shifts we are seeing and the anxiety many feel. We need something tangible to hold our thoughts on the shifts in culture, and we have chosen phones. Thus, the clash over phones today might be between those who think in mythic/symbolic ways, and those who think in more scientific ways.

Anonymous, quoted by [Tyler Cowen, Marginal Revolution](https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2026/02/a-new-hypothesis-from-my-email.html?utm_source=rss&utm_medium=rss&utm_campaign=a-new-hypothesis-from-my-email).
